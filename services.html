Our Services

We specialise in helping learners pass their practical driving test with confidence. Below are the key services we offer:

Lesson Prices

We offer flexible lesson packages for manual and automatic learners. Options range from hourly bookings to full intensive courses. All pricing is transparent — no hidden fees.

Car for Practical Test

Need a vehicle for your test? We provide clean, dual-controlled cars that meet DVSA requirements. You’ll take the test in the same car you’ve trained in — no surprises.

Become an Instructor

If you're looking to start a new career, we offer instructor training with structured ADI preparation. Learn from experienced professionals and get DVSA test-ready.

Refresher Lessons

Been off the road for a while? We offer refresher courses tailored to your level — regain confidence behind the wheel without pressure.
