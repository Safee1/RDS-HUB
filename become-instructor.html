Become a Driving Instructor

Want to become your own boss and start a new career? Join our instructor training and franchise program designed for serious, motivated individuals who want to earn well and work independently.

Why Join Us?

  • No upfront training fees
  • No binding legal contracts
  • Work when and where you want
  • Choose your own tuition vehicle
  • We handle your marketing
  • We book your initial lessons
  • Franchise includes 2 weeks free per year
  • Group meetings to stay sharp on standards
  • Standards check prep and ongoing support

Who We're Looking For

Experienced instructors looking for steady work, or complete beginners ready to qualify. You'll need the right mindset: reliable, motivated, good with people, and serious about teaching.

What We Offer

We provide full ADI Part 1, 2, and 3 training. You’ll get one-to-one coaching, flexible scheduling, business guidance, and the tools you need to pass and succeed.

Areas We Cover (and Growing):

We currently serve Birmingham, Wolverhampton, Walsall, and Ilford — with more locations coming soon as we continue to expand.
