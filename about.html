About Rana Driving School

Confidence You Can See. Results You Can Feel.

At Rana Driving School, we’re more than instructors—we’re mentors for the road. With over 25 years of combined experience, our team has helped 1000s of learners become test-ready, road-safe, and confident drivers for life.

Founded by Mr. Rana, a local driving instructor with over 22 years behind the wheel, our school combines technical knowledge with a calm, adaptable approach. In 2021, after completing a First Class Honours degree, I joined the business full-time to bring a modern, structured approach to driver training.

From your first lesson, you’ll get a personalised experience designed to build confidence, independence, and safety. Our DVSA-qualified instructors are patient, friendly, and know the test routes inside out.

Whether you’re a complete beginner or switching from manual to automatic, we’ll help you get test-ready faster—with flexible lesson scheduling, progress tracking, and full support through every stage.
