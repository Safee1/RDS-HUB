About Rana Driving School

Experience You Can Trust — Progress You Can Feel

At Rana Driving School, we bring over two decades of hands-on driving instruction backed by deep local knowledge and a commitment to real results.

The school was founded by Mr. Rana, who’s been teaching learners for over 22 years, building a reputation for calm, effective, and adaptable instruction. In 2021, I officially joined the business after learning the ropes alongside my studies. I now hold a First Class Honours degree, and I’m combining my technical background with driving education to modernise how we teach and operate.

From day one, our focus has been simple: help learners become safe, confident, and independent drivers — whether you're just starting out or switching to automatic. Our teaching methods are structured, DVSA-compliant, and tailored to individual needs.

We cover all major test centres in the region and offer flexible lesson times, mock tests, progress tracking, and practical feedback after every session.

Why Choose Us?

  • Over 25 years combined experience
  • Manual and automatic instructors
  • Flexible pick-up and drop-off
  • Patient, multilingual, DVSA-approved trainers
  • Local route knowledge that gives you the edge

Whether you’re starting from zero or returning after a break, Rana Driving School will get you test-ready — and road-ready.
